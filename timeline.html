SUPRA 2012

AIR-1: Weight event of Formula SAE-India in 2012. Only team among the teams participating for the first time to qualify the virtuals round.

They say that the journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step. Under the leadership of Charles Peter, the foundation of that journey was laid. Then known as Team VEGA, the team happened to be the only team among all those teams who were participating in the event for the first time to have successfully cleared the virtuals round.






BAJA SAE INDIA 2013

2013: AIR-84 BAJA SAE-INDIA standing ahead of IIT Kanpur.

Taking that step forward, Team VEGA then tested the hot waters of BAJA SAE, which happens to be one of the most reputed competitions all over the nation. Team VEGA, though still a novice, left some colleges behind including IIT Kanpur. BAJA SAE India was a life changing experience for Team VEGA. The bar was now to be raised significantly higher.






BAJA Student India 2014-15

AIR-28 BAJA STUDENT INDIA amongst other 36 participating teams.
AIR-5: Sales event of Baja Student India in 2015.

The BSI of 2014-15 was a great learning experience for the team. The team bagged 28th position all over India showing an immense growth in performance. The team surprised the crowd by securing 5th position in the sales event.





Mega ATV Championship 2016

AIR-5: Overall placing of Mega ATV Championship in 2016

Mega ATV Championship happened to be more of a dynamic event than a design oriented one. The robust vehicle that the team made ensured that it performed top notch throughout.






Enduro Student India 2017

AIR 4 in Endurance event.
AIR 14 in overall event.

Enduro Student India was the competition in which Team VEGA finally performed to its true abilities. Relentless testing, thorough analysis and calculated designing ensured that the beast of an ATV they created came out with flying colors in the competition.Not only did MARK-I dominate the race track throughout but it stood on 4th position, overall fetching a 14th position.







Current Project

Team VEGA Racing aspires to be in the top three positions next year. Any vehicle is one part innovation, one part cooperation and one part competition. This year, the team will leave no stone unturned to find the optimum balance among all of those elements and create the best performing All Terrain vehicle.
